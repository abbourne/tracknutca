About TrackNut

Welcome to Track Nut! We’re a couple of, well old, guys from Ontario that have re-discovered the joy of track cycling after over 30 years absence from the track. With the construction of the amazing Mattamy National Cycling Centre in 2015, we couldn’t resist the lure of a UCI class 1 velodrome on our doorstep. We’re generally on the track multiple times a week.
